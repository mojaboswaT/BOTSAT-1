What is BOTSAT-1?

BOTSAT-1 is Botswana's first satellite, launched to enhance the country's capabilities in space technology. With its advanced sensors and imaging systems, the satellite plays a crucial role in collecting data that supports various national sectors.

BOTSAT-1 provides critical data for agriculture, climate monitoring, disaster response, and national development. By enhancing crop yields, tracking climate change, and improving disaster management, it strengthens Botswana's position in the global space community and contributes to sustainable development.

With BOTSAT-1, Botswana is now better equipped to address the challenges posed by climate change, optimize resource use, and ensure food security for its people. It represents a giant leap forward in the country's technological evolution and its ability to contribute to international space efforts.
